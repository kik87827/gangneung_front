The ITS PARK

This the Gangneung Olympic Park to be transformed as The ITS Park

As the venue for the 2018 PyeongChang Winter Olympics, Gangneung Olympic Park was completed in 2017 and has since been utilized as a multipurpose space for sports and international events. Beyond hosting the 2026 ITS World Congress, it is set to transform into the epicenter of the global ITS festival
