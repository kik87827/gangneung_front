Express Bus

Taking an express bus is a convenient way to get to Gangneung from Seoul.
You can take an express bus from the Express Bus Terminal or Dong Seoul Terminal.
It is convenient to take the subway from the airport to get to either of the two bus terminals. The Express Bus Terminal is located on subway lines 3, 7 and 9 (Express Bus Terminal Station), while the Dong Seoul Terminal is located on subway line 2 (Gangbyeon Station).

  • Incheon International Airport
  • Seoul subway
  • Dong Seoul Bus Terminal or Seoul Express Bus Terminal
  • Gangneung Express Bus Terminal

Taxi

Taxis in Gangneung are considered an affordable and convenient means of transportation. You can catch a taxi anywhere in Gangneung on the streets. All the taxis charge the fare based on a metering system, with additional charges imposed upon the base fare of KRW 3,800 according to the distance of travel. Taxi drivers will accept payment in cash or by credit card.
